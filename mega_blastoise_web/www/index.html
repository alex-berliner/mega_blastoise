HOW TO PLAY

OVERVIEW

Gen 1 random battle. Each player gets 3 random Pokémon. Last team standing wins.

STARTING

Press any button to ready up. Battle begins once both players are ready.

EACH TURN

Both players choose simultaneously — your opponent can't see your pick. After both choose, the turn resolves.

Tap any button within 1 second of choosing to cancel and reselect.

MOVE BUTTONS

TapSelect that move
HoldView move details (type, power, accuracy, PP) — release to go back

SWITCH BUTTONS S1 S2 S3

TapSwitch to that party slot
HoldCycle through party stats pages — release to go back

LEDS

HP bar (8)Current HP — green > 50%, yellow > 25%, red ≤ 25%
Party (3)Alive / fainted status for each Pokémon

BUTTONS

BLUE VS AIBlue player controlled by AI
DEMOBoth players AI-controlled
PAUSE / RESUMEPause AI between turns
RESTARTStart a new battle
RESETFull page reload

CLI

The log below mirrors the battle narration. Type commands and press Enter.

:vs aiBlue (you) vs AI opponent
:readyBoth players ready (human vs human)
:demoAI vs AI demo loop
:restartStart a new battle
